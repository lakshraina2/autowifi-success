Privacy Policy for AutoWifi Login

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the AutoWifi Login extension collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect any personal data.

The AutoWifi Login extension is designed to function entirely locally on your device.

  • Credentials: Your Wi-Fi username and password are saved strictly to your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any external server owned by the developer.
  • Browsing Activity: The extension monitors URLs solely for the purpose of detecting the captive portal login page (specifically 172.16.x.x gateways). It does not track, store, or transmit your browsing history.

2. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools.

3. Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions for functionality only:

  • Host Permissions: Required to detect the network login page redirection on any website.
  • Scripting: Required to automatically fill in your saved credentials on the login page.
  • Storage: Required to save your preferences and credentials locally.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.
